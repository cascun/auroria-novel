About the Book

Thirteen-year-old Gordy Jones has spent most of his life trying not to be noticed. Bullied at school and lonely at home, he reaches a breaking point one night and wishes the world would disappear.

Instead, the world opens.

Beyond the tear in reality waits Auroria, a secret place of silver trees, glowing skies, golden birds, and impossible freedom. But when one of Gordy’s bullies is pulled into the world, something ancient begins to wake.

To save Auroria, Gordy must face the boys who hurt him, the darkness he awakened, and the hardest truth of all: making a world is easy. Taking care of one is not.

A coming-of-age fantasy for readers who enjoy magical worlds, unlikely friendships, emotional adventure, and stories about courage, loneliness, and belonging.

About the Author

J. M. Mifsud writes fantasy stories about hidden worlds, quiet courage, and the strange magic of ordinary people. The Boy Who Made a World is his first Auroria novel.
